## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo

The NEXORA logo uses:
- **Geometric form**: Multi-faceted design representing AI complexity
- **Color**: Blue 800 (#1E40AF)
- **Space**: Clear zone of 20px minimum around all sides
- **Size**: Minimum 48px width on web, 32px on mobile

### Logo Variants

```
1. Horizontal Logo
   └─ Full-width format, suitable for headers
   └─ Minimum width: 240px

2. Stacked Logo
   └─ Logo above wordmark, suitable for social profiles
   └─ Minimum width: 120px

3. Icon Only (Favicon)
   └─ Logo mark without wordmark
   └─ Minimum size: 32px × 32px

4. Inverse (White on Dark)
   └─ For dark backgrounds
   └─ Maintain minimum contrast 3:1
```

### Clear Space

```
         20px
      ┌────────────┐
   20 │            │ 20
    px│   NEXORA   │ px
      │            │
      └────────────┘
         20px
```

Always maintain minimum 20px clear space around logo.

### Incorrect Usage

❌ Never stretch or distort
❌ Never rotate at angles other than 90°
❌ Never use colors other than approved palette
❌ Never add effects (drop shadow, glow) without approval
❌ Never place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

### File Formats

- **Web**: SVG (preferred), PNG with transparency
- **Print**: PDF vectors
- **Social**: PNG 1200×630px, background transparent
- **Favicon**: ICO, PNG 32×32px

**Blue 800 (#1E40AF)**

Use for:

```
✅ GOOD                    ❌ BAD
Blue + White              Blue + Gray
Blue + Amber              Blue + Lime
White + Gray              Gray + Gray (low contrast)
Amber + Dark Blue         Amber + Orange (too similar)
```

## Typography

### Font: Inter

We exclusively use **Inter** (open-source typeface).

- **Desktop**: Embed via Google Fonts or Typekit
- **Mobile Apps**: System fonts may substitute
- **Fallback**: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif`

### Typeface Rules

- **Headings**: Bold (700) or Semi-bold (600)
- **Body**: Regular (400) or Medium (500)
- **Emphasis**: Italic or underline, not color alone
- **Code**: Monospace font (Monaco, Courier New)

### Line Length

- **Optimal**: 50-75 characters per line
- **Maximum**: 100 characters (avoid longer)
- **Mobile**: Single column, full width allowed

## Voice & Tone

### NEXORA Voice

**Intelligent, Innovative, Approachable, Trustworthy**

We speak as industry experts who make complex ideas simple.

### Tone Variations

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Marketing | Inspiring, aspirational | "Transform your brand in minutes, not months" |
| Onboarding | Helpful, supportive | "Let's get your brand set up. We'll walk through this together." |
| Error messages | Clear, actionable | "We couldn't save your changes. Check your internet and try again." |
| Documentation | Technical, precise | "Deploy using `docker compose up -d` to start PostgreSQL." |
| Support | Empathetic, solution-focused | "We understand this is frustrating. Here's how we'll fix it:" |

### Writing Guidelines

✅ **DO:**
- Use active voice ("We generate logos" not "Logos are generated")
- Keep sentences short (15-20 words average)
- Use contractions ("We're" not "We are")
- Address the reader directly ("You can create..." not "One can create...")
- Use specific benefits ("Save 10 hours per week" not "Save time")

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use jargon without explanation
- Write in passive voice
- Use ALL CAPS for emphasis (use **bold** instead)
- Anthropomorphize AI ("The AI thinks..." → "The system...")
- Make unrealistic promises

### Example Messaging

```
❌ WEAK: "Our product helps with brand management"
✅ STRONG: "Create professional brand identities in 10 minutes—even if you've never designed before"

❌ WEAK: "We have AI-powered features"
✅ STRONG: "Four AI specialists (Brand, Design, Content, Research) work together on your project"

❌ WEAK: "Enterprise security"
✅ STRONG: "Bank-level encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and audit logging of every change"
```

## Partnership & Referral Guidelines

### Co-branding

When creating co-branded materials:
1. Use both logos, equal size
2. Include "NEXORA and [Partner]" clearly
3. Maintain clear space between logos
4. Get approval from both parties before publication

### Referral Program

**Tagline**: "Recommend NEXORA and earn rewards"

- Referrer gets $500 credit per conversion
- Partner gets 20% lifetime discount
- No limits on referrals

### Affiliate Guidelines

- Use approved banners (provided in partner portal)
- Disclose affiliate relationship clearly
- Don't claim NEXORA endorsement
- Direct links to landing pages only

## Brand Prohibitions

### NEVER:

❌ Use NEXORA name as verb ("to NEXORA a project")

❌ Use unbranded or unofficial materials
